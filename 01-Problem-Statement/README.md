# Digital Banking Case Study  
## Improving Customer Onboarding & KYC Journey  

### 📌 Overview
This case study demonstrates end-to-end product ownership of a digital onboarding transformation initiative in a retail banking environment.

The objective was to reduce onboarding friction, improve regulatory compliance, and increase activation rates while maintaining strict KYC controls.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

- 38% onboarding drop-off rate
- Manual KYC review causing 48-hour delay
- Poor mobile UX
- Regulatory risk exposure

---

## 👥 Target Users

- Retail banking customers (18–55)
- SME customers
- Internal compliance & risk teams

---

## 🚀 Product Goals

- Reduce onboarding drop-off from 38% → 20%
- Reduce KYC verification time from 48h → <10 minutes
- Improve mobile completion rate by 25%
- Maintain full FCA compliance

---

## 🧠 My Role as Product Owner

- Defined product vision & roadmap
- Created Epics & User Stories
- Prioritised backlog using RICE
- Collaborated with Risk & Legal teams
- Led sprint planning & backlog refinement
- Managed UAT & stakeholder sign-off

---

## 📊 Key Outcomes (Simulated)

- 22% increase in completed applications
- 35% reduction in manual review cases
- 18% improvement in customer activation
- 30% faster onboarding cycle time
